Educational Platform

This platform; By focusing on visualization of educational content, related content can be used as games, animations, gamification, etc. It will be in a way that will enable the creation of high-quality and easy-to-learn content in the field of education.

Our main goals in this project are;

  • Contributing to the digitalization of educational content,
  • To contribute to the easier, faster and permanent learning process of people by providing visualization of educational content.
  • Contents animations, gamifications, etc. and to increase efficiency by making the boring educational process more interactive in this way.
  • Creating a new developer/creator ecosystem and increasing educational content in the game and graphics industry.
